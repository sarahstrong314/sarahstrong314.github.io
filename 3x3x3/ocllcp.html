Corners of the Last Layer (Preserving Edge Orientation)

Printable Version

OCLLCP, commonly known as COLL, involves solving LL corners and preserving the orientation of the LL edges. It's used to for CP skips when you're doing OCLL. Most of these algorithms are from Dan Cohen's site and Jason Baum's site, and the rest are from a few other pages.
