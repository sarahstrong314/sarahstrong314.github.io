Corner step of the Last Slot

Printable Version

CLS is part of the MGLS method. CLS involves placing the LS corner while orienting the LL corners. It is used when the LS edge is in place and the LL edges are oriented. The algorithms are from Lucas Garron's site, and the alg names under each image are from his naming scheme.
