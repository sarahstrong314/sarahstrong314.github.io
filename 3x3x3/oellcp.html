Orientation of the Edges of the Last Layer and Corner Permutation

Printable Version

OELLCP is an add-on to CFOP. When you're on the OLL step and you have an OELL case (OLL # 20, 28 or 57), which is an OLL case where the corners are oriented but either 2 or 4 edges are ' flipped', you can use a OELLCP alg. This would orient the edges, completing the OLL, while permuting the corners. This would leave you with an EPLL (U, Z or H perm) or a solved last layer. OELLCP is a subset of OLLCP, just like COLL. It is useful because EPLLs are among the easiest PLLs, for both recognition and execution speed. The chance of getting a PLL skip is 1 in 12 when you have an EOLL case and you use a OELLCP to solve it. There are only 13 cases for this, so I'd say it's worth learning.

  • M' U M U2 M' U M
  • L'w U R' U2 L U' L' U2 R2 x'
  • x R2 U2 L U L' U2 R U' Lw
  • Rw U' L U2 R' U R U2 L2 x'
  • x L2 U2 R' U' R U2 L' U R'w
  • R' U R U y R2 D L' B' L D' R2
  • R U R' U' M' U R U' R'w
  • x' M' U' R U M U2 L2 U R' U' L2 U2 x U
  • R U' R' F' U F R x' U' R U R'2 x
  • R U' R'2 F R F' R U' x' U F2 U' R' x
  • Rw U R' U' M'2 U R U' R' U' M'
  • x' U L' U L2 x U L' U' x' U2 F' R U' R' U x
  • x' U R' F2 U2 R' U R2 U' R U2 F2 R U' x
