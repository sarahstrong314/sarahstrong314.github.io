Edge Permutation

Printable Version

The algs are from Lars Vandenbergh's site, this thread, and from videos on David Woner's and Bingliang Li's YouTube channel.

Red text indicates a case with parity, and an asterisk indicated that the equator will be flipped afterthe alg is performed.

The numbering system is as followed:

0: all edges permuted, 1: adjacent swap, 2: opposite swap, 3: clockwise O perm, 4: counterclockwise O perm, 5: W perm, 6: clockwise U perm, 7: counterclockwise U perm, 8: Z perm, 9: H perm.

The first digit indicates the cycle on the D layer and the second digit indicates the cycle on the U layer.
